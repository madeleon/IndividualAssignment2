Summary

        C3, Inc., doing business as C3 IoT, develops and delivers enterprise software solutions. The company offers C3 IoT Platform, a Platform-as-a-Service solution that enables the rapid design, development, deployment, and operation of enterprise-scale software applications. It also provides C3 CRM that clarifies and optimizes decisions about customer-facing actions; C3 Predictive Maintenance, a solution that predicts and diagnoses equipment or system failures; C3 Capital Asset Planning, a tool that executes precise, data-driven asset plans; C3 Enterprise, a solution that enable large commercial and industrial customers to understand and manage energy performance and savings; C3 Fraud Detection, an application that pinpoints and mitigates fraud activity; and C3 Supply Network Risk, a solution to optimize the end-to-end supply network.

        In addition, the company offers C3 Sensor Health, a solution to maximize device/system productivity and operations; C3 Health Score Analytics, a solution to evaluate and address consumer health risks and manage costs; C3 Vehicle Fleet Analytics, a tool to prioritize and optimize vehicle fleet maintenance; C3 Energy Grid Analytics, an application to optimize the investing, operational, and demand response activities; C3 Energy Customer Analytics, an application that transforms utilities interaction with their customers; and C3 Energy Oil & Gas Analytics applications to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of decision-making from well development and production, to pipeline transportation and downstream operations.
